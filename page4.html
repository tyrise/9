Story

This is a fiction story about greed and inequality in Nigeria and the efforts of the government to eradicate it as part of their cardinal agenda. A taskforce within the National Security Service (Persons of Interest Unit) was set up reporting directly to the vice President Amaka Nnadi.

The P.O.I Unit is made up of 5 officers headed by Hamza Daniyal, by the book, light hearted but serious trained in US, Michael Obi Price (a tough high tech officer trained in the UK) and 2 undercover agents, Stephanie Okafor, a famous event planner (trained in the UK and a weapons expert) and Zainab Suleiman (Gym/fitness owner and martial arts expert. Trained in Nigeria and US.) The 5th officer is our suspense character for our surprise element later!

A data base which is linked to other international POI data system sources out classified intel and assignments for the Taskforce. Kiki Williams (UK trained) is the IT specialist who networks with her international counterparts to source or share intel. She briefs the team about any new assignment and sources intel to support any investigation.

The Don one of the names generated by the POI data system is a politician and family man. He is likeable and a manipulator; His shady government and business affairs brought him in the radar of the P.O.I Unit. The story continues in each episode with new assignments to the POI Unit to investigate and apprehend suspects.

Disclaimer: This fiction story is not related to anybody, living or dead!
